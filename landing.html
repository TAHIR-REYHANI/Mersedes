About the company

The choice of the name Mercedes in Germany goes back to a very wealthy businessman named Emil Jelnik,
and this man was the founder of racing cars at this period of time and he had a daughter named Mercedes Emil Jelnik.

MERCEDES BENZ

It is a German manufacturer of luxury cars, buses and trucks. It is the oldest automobile company in the world, with its origins going back to Karl Benz's creation of the first car in January 1886.Months after this achievement, Gottlieb Daimler and engineer Wilhelm Maybach also produced another car, and in 1901 the first Mercedes was produced. In 1926, the Karl-Benz and Gottlieb Daimler companies merged to become Daimler-Benz, which produced the first Mercedes-Benz cars.[1] Mercedes-Benz has, over the years, been famous for introducing many innovations in technology and safety that later became a constant feature of all contemporary cars.

Mercedes company

Mercedes-Benz is a German car manufacturing brand, founded in 1926 by Karl Benz, Gottlieb Daimler, Wilhelm Maybach and Emil Jellinek. Today, the company has 93 locations around the world, and its headquarters is located in Stuttgart, Germany, and its factories are spread in 17 countries around the world spread over five continents.

Mercedes history

Mercedes-Benz was founded initially in the name of Daimler-Benz after the names of its founders Karl Benz and Gottlieb Daimler. Benz launched the first gasoline-powered car, while Daimler patented the first motorcycle and later built his own car, both of which were designed with the help of Wilhelm Maybach, who is also an imprint in the field of industries cars, but the history of Mercedes dates back to before the day it was founded.

Mercedes products

Mercedes-Benz AG is responsible for the business of Mercedes-Benz cars, Mercedes-Benz trucks and Mercedes-Benz Vans, has more than 170,000 employees in its team, and its products include Mercedes-Benz cars and other sub-brands.

Information and facts about Mercedes

Here are some information and facts about Mercedes-Benz: Mercedes built a hybrid car in 1906 called the Mercedes Mixte with a top speed of 75 miles per hour, and this car was equipped with a petrol engine and electric dynamo for the rear wheels. Mercedes-Benz created anti-lock brakes specifically for the S-Class in 1978, a feature that helps maintain the vehicle's steering without locking the wheels in the event of emergency braking. Mercedes-Benz invented the self-driving car in 1995, when this feature was a fantasy, in its S-class cars, which managed to go without a driver 990 miles (about 1,593 km) from Munich, Germany, to Copenhagen, Denmark, and back. The Kempinski Hotel has contracted with Mercedes to provide its customers with a first-class transfer service with its various vehicles, between sports, limousines and SUVs. The Mercedes-Maybach S650 Convertible is the most expensive Mercedes car. In 2017, Mercedes-Benz achieved a record number of cars sold that year, which amounted to 2.4 million cars, and this year was the eighth consecutive year that Mercedes achieved a record number of sales. China is the largest market for Mercedes-Benz cars with 28% of vehicle sales, followed by the USA with 14% and Germany with 14% as well.
