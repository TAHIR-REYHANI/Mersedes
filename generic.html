About the car

Mercedes-Benz is one of the cars produced by Mercedes, which is one of the leading companies in the automotive industry and offers a wide variety of items it produces. Manufacturing, as it is rare to issue a category that has workmanship defects.

The various Mercedes classes have engines with a relatively large capacity, and a power commensurate with the size and weight of the car, allowing it to accelerate and reach the top speed in the least possible time. What distinguishes Mercedes engines is their ability to withstand high speeds of rotation (noise). Mercedes in all classes uses the best safety systems of its own. Provides airbags in the cockpit at various angles to protect the driver and passengers, during traffic accidents. As for the suspension system, there are sensors on all wheels of the car to adjust and redistribute pressure to avoid slipping or deviating from the track. As well as the anti-lock brake system and the system to walk on ice and snow.

The negative aspects of a Mercedes-Benz are not related to the quality of the car or its specifications, but rather to its high price compared to other types. The cost of maintenance and additions is very high, which causes a burden on those who own it. The great power of Mercedes engines robs them of the feature of economical consumption, and therefore when comparing each category with its counterparts from other cars, we find that Mercedes consumes fuel at a greater rate.
